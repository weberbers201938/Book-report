Book Report: Holes by Louis Sachar

I. Title

Holes

II. Author

Louis Sachar

III. Genre

Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

IV. Summary

Holes is about a boy named Stanley Yelnats who is sent to a juvenile correctional camp...

V. Setting

  • Camp Green Lake
  • The Texas Desert

VI. Characters

    Major Characters:

  • Stanley Yelnats
  • Hector Zeroni (Zero)
  • The Warden

    • Minor Characters:

  • Mr. Sir
  • Mr. Pendanski
  • X-Ray
  • Kate Barlow

VII. Plot

  1. Stanley Yelnats is sent to Camp Green Lake for a crime he didn’t commit.
  2. The boys at the camp are forced to dig holes every day.
  3. Stanley befriends a boy named Zero, and they work together.
  4. They discover that the warden is searching for hidden treasure.
  5. Stanley and Zero find the treasure and break the Yelnats family curse.

VIII. Moral Lesson

The story teaches the importance of friendship, perseverance, and justice. It also shows that standing up for what is right, even in difficult situations, can lead to great outcomes.
