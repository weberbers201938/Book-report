Holes by Louis Sachar

I. Title

Holes

II. Author

Louis Sachar

III. Setting

When: Modern times

Where: Camp Green Lake, Texas

IV. Characters

Major Characters:

  • Stanley Yelnats
  • Hector Zeroni (Zero)
  • The Warden

Minor Characters:

  • Mr. Sir
  • Mr. Pendanski
  • X-Ray
  • Kate Barlow

V. Plot

  1. Stanley Yelnats is sent to Camp Green Lake for a crime he didn’t commit.
  2. The boys at the camp are forced to dig holes every day.
  3. Stanley befriends a boy named Zero, and they work together.
  4. They discover that the warden is searching for hidden treasure.
  5. Stanley and Zero find the treasure and break the Yelnats family curse.

VI. Moral Lesson

The story teaches the importance of friendship, perseverance, and justice. It also shows that standing up for what is right, even in difficult situations, can lead to positive change.
